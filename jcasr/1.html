The Education Technology Unit (ETU), established in 1996, is dedicated to enhancing the quality of education through the development of innovative teaching and learning resources. The primary goal of the unit is to create interactive and engaging materials that support educators and learners in understanding complex scientific concepts. ETU specializes in producing multimedia packages, including CD-ROMs, in various scientific disciplines. These resources are carefully designed to cater to the needs of students and faculty, promoting a deeper understanding of the subjects. In addition to multimedia tools, the unit has also developed an array of books, both in English and regional Indian languages, to broaden accessibility and inclusivity. Over the years, ETU has played a pivotal role in bridging the gap between traditional teaching methods and modern educational technology, contributing significantly to the academic excellence of the university.

The end-users of these multimedia packages were identified as students in high schools and colleges.
