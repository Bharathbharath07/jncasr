facilities

Computer facilities are available for creating graphics/images, animations, voice recordings, and compressing and converting movie files for integration into multimedia projects.

All development work, including scripting, incorporating graphics, movies, and sound elements into multimedia formats, is done in-house.

We offer extensive Desktop Publishing (DTP) services to produce print-ready materials for books, brochures, and other promotional content—all completed in-house.

Our chemistry laboratory is equipped to conduct experiments using low-cost college chemistry kits.

SOP-POCE's Chemistry laboratory is designed to accommodate 24-25 students for general chemistry experiments in a single session.

Viewing Room: This space is used for viewing multimedia presentations/CD-ROM titles developed by ETU, video lectures by Professor CNR Rao, and other public lectures

Professor CNR Rao Archives: Honoris Causa Doctorate degrees awarded to Professor CNR Rao, along with Medals, Awards, and other honors from national and international universities, are displayed in the Archives.

The Chemistry of Materials Exposition showcases Panels, Books, and Models of simple molecules and complex structures.

The Madan Mohan Malaviya Amphitheatre is located in the Hall of Science building.
